About Me

Welcome to my personal portfolio! I'm a front-end developer with a passion for crafting engaging and intuitive websites. With a background in data science modules, I blend technical expertise with a keen eye for design to create seamless digital experiences. Beyond the code, I'm an avid film photographer. I find inspiration in the world around me, capturing moments on film that tell unique stories. This portfolio not only highlights my web development skills but also showcases my journey in film photography. Explore my work and join me in celebrating the intersection of technology and art.
